Scrumptious Chocolate Brownies!

The most succulent brownies in South Africa!

Ingredients:
  • 2 cups white sugar
  • 1 cup butter
  • 1/2 cup cocoa
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 4 eggs
  • 1 1/2 cups flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup walnuts

Directions:

Melt butter.
Mix all wet ingredients together.
Combine wet and dry ingredients in the order given.
Bake at 350 degrees farenheit (180 degrees celcius) for 20 - 30 minutes.
